Quadruped Robot

Description

The Project focuses on developing a quadruped (four-legged) robot capable of straight walking, turning,
crossing obstacles, and slope climbing. The leg comprises a five-bar mechanism that requires two rotary
actuators to perform the leg movement. The Quadruped Robot can perform various activities such as
walking at various speeds, turning in both directions at different angles and can walk on slopes also.

Project start date : Oct 2018

Project end date : July 2019
